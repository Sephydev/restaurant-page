About us

The world has already lost too much.

Cities. Memories. Lives.

Here at the Clock Tower Refuge, no one fights.
We lay down our weapons, sit down… and eat a hot meal.

Our chicken curry is slow-cooked, with spices that warm the body as much as the soul. In the ruins of the Sunken City, I learned one thing: a shared meal can save a day… sometimes even a life.

As long as hope exists, there will always be a place to find strength.
